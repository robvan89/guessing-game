Let's Play a Game

Here's what this script is doing:

The initial prompt asks the player for their name and logging its value into the console.

First, it's declaring a variable for the player's score.

Then, it gives the player a prompt involving a yes or no question.

  • The toLowerCase() function is used in case the player uses capitalization when inputting their answers.

    • The resulting lower-case string is assigned to a variable, which is then checked against the answer (either yes or no) for that question.

    If the answer is correct, the score variable is increased by 1 and the script moves on to the next question.

  • (Otherwise, the script adds the question to the missed variable and moves on to the next question.)

    • The remaining questions are fired off, and their answers are checked.

    After all questions have been answered, an alert pops up displaying the player's final score.

  • The message at the end of the alert varies depending on how well the player did.

    • The page displays the player's final score where the text in the nukeit div was.
